Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026 · Operator: PerkWorth Technologies Pvt. Ltd. · Contact: support@perkworth.com

This Privacy Policy explains how PerkWorth ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, and shares your information when you use our mobile and web application ("Service"). By using PerkWorth you agree to this Policy. If you do not agree, please uninstall the App.

0. Data Protection Clause

Your wallet data is encrypted. All vouchers, points, memberships and family-circle records are transmitted over HTTPS / TLS 1.3 and stored in encrypted MongoDB Atlas (AES-256 at rest). Your password is one-way hashed with bcrypt — even our engineers cannot read it.

We never sell, rent, share, or trade your financial data. PerkWorth's only revenue source is the ₹99/quarter Pro membership. We do not run ads, we do not profile you, we do not work with data brokers, and we do not share your wallet with advertisers under any circumstance.

You may erase everything, instantly. Open the app → Settings → Clear All My Data. This deletes your account, your wallet, your payments history, and your referral records server-side immediately. Backups are purged within 30 days.

1. Information We Collect

  • You provide: Email, name, phone (optional), vouchers/codes/expiries you save.
  • SMS (Android only, with consent): Filtered on-device for shopping keywords ("₹ off", "voucher", "code", "expires", "points", "loyalty"). Only the SMS you tap “Save” reaches our backend.
  • Payments: Razorpay processes payments. We retain only order_id, payment_id, and HMAC-SHA256 signature for verification — never your card number, UPI ID, or bank credentials.

2. What We Do NOT Collect

✗ Bank OTPs · ✗ Personal chats · ✗ Contacts · ✗ Call logs · ✗ Location · ✗ Browsing history · ✗ Biometrics · ✗ Government IDs.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To operate the Service: store and retrieve your vouchers, points, memberships, and family-circle records.
  • To process payments via Razorpay for the Pro membership.
  • To notify you (in-app + optional browser push) of expiring vouchers, ROI break-evens, and program changes from our daily Market Intelligence cron.
  • To improve the Service (aggregated, anonymous usage patterns only).

4. Sub-processors

  • OpenAI / Emergent — OCR & SMS parsing (data-processing-only, no model training).
  • Razorpay — PCI-DSS Level 1 payment processor.
  • MongoDB Atlas — encrypted database hosting (Mumbai region).

5. Your rights — DPDP 2023 (India)

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, you have the right to access (§11), correction (§12), erasure (§13), and grievance redressal (§13(3)) of your personal data.

  • Access: All wallet data is visible in-app. Use Settings → Privacy Control → Export my wallet (JSON or CSV) for a downloadable copy.
  • Erasure: Settings → Clear All My Data deletes your account and ALL server-side records immediately. Backups are purged within 30 days.
  • Grievance Officer: grievance@perkworth.com — statutory response within 30 days.

6. Your rights — GDPR (EU / EEA / UK)

If you are in the EEA / UK, GDPR Articles 15–22 apply, including:

  • Art. 15 — Right of access
  • Art. 16 — Right to rectification
  • Art. 17 — Right to erasure (“right to be forgotten”)
  • Art. 18 — Right to restriction of processing
  • Art. 20 — Right to data portability (use the JSON / CSV export)
  • Art. 21 — Right to object
  • Art. 22 — No solely-automated decisions

Lawful basis: consent (SMS scanning), contract (Pro membership), legitimate interest (fraud prevention on payments). DPO contact: dpo@perkworth.com.

7. Retention & Deletion

We retain your data only as long as your account is active. Tap Clear All My Data to delete everything immediately. Database backups are purged within 30 days.

8. Children

PerkWorth is not intended for users below 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has registered, email support@perkworth.com and we will erase the account.

9. Changes to this Policy

We will notify you in-app at least 30 days before any material change to this Policy. Your continued use after the change confirms acceptance.
