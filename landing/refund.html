Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: February 2026 · Applies to: PerkWorth Pro Quarterly subscription (₹99 every 3 months) processed via Razorpay.

1. Nature of the Service

PerkWorth Pro is a digital, subscription-based service. The moment your payment is captured by Razorpay, you receive immediate access to all Pro features (unlimited storage, AI scanning, Family Circle, Perk Tips, ROI tracker, etc.) — including features that consume compute (OpenAI extractions, Razorpay processing).

2. Refund Policy

Once the Pro membership is activated, the ₹99 quarterly fee is non-refundable.

This is because:

  • You receive instant access to digital benefits at the moment of activation.
  • Several Pro features (AI image scans, GPT-4o-powered Perk Tips, optimizer suggestions) consume per-call compute we are billed for irrespective of how long the subscription was used.
  • Razorpay processing fees are non-recoverable from the gateway after capture.

This non-refund policy is consistent with the standard practice for digital subscriptions in India and with industry norms for sub-₹500/quarter SaaS products.

3. Cancellation of Auto-Renewal

You can cancel your auto-renewal at any time directly from inside the app:

  1. Open the PerkWorth app.
  2. Go to Profile menu → Membership.
  3. Tap Cancel auto-renewal.

After cancellation:

  • You retain Pro access for the remainder of the already-paid quarter.
  • No further automatic charges are made on the next renewal date.
  • At the end of the quarter, your account silently downgrades to the Free tier — your wallet, vouchers, and circle members are preserved; only Pro-gated features become read-only.

You can re-subscribe anytime by paying ₹99 again.

4. Exceptions — when we WILL refund

Notwithstanding §2, we will issue a full refund in the following cases:

  • Duplicate / accidental charge: If Razorpay charges you twice for the same upgrade attempt due to a technical glitch.
  • Failure to deliver the service: If, due to a PerkWorth-side outage lasting more than 7 consecutive days, you are unable to access core Pro features.
  • Unauthorized payment: If your account was charged without your authorization and you report it to support@perkworth.app within 7 days of the charge.
  • Mistaken payment after cancellation: If a renewal charge slipped through after you cancelled auto-renewal in-app.

5. How to request a refund (exceptional cases only)

  1. Email support@perkworth.app from the email associated with your PerkWorth account.
  2. Include: the Razorpay order_id or payment_id, the date of charge, and the reason (duplicate / outage / unauthorized / mistaken renewal).
  3. We acknowledge within 2 business days and either approve or explain rejection within 7 business days.
  4. Approved refunds are processed via Razorpay back to the original payment method. Bank settlement typically takes 5–10 business days.

6. Right to delete your data

Cancellation of auto-renewal does not delete your data. If you also wish to delete your account and all stored data, use the in-app Settings → Clear All My Data flow (DPDP 2023 §13 / GDPR Art. 17). This is independent of the refund process.

7. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Refund Policy. Material changes will be notified in-app at least 30 days before they take effect. The version in force at the time of your charge applies to that charge.
